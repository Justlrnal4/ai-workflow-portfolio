# Spatial Limen

## Summary

Spatial Limen is an AI literature discovery and research mapping platform.

## Problem

Research discovery is difficult when important ideas are scattered across many papers, fields, and citation paths.

## What I built

A research discovery concept using a graph-based corpus to explore relationships between papers, ideas, and research areas.

## Stack

Graph data modeling, AI-assisted research workflows, search and discovery concepts, and complex information architecture.

## What is public vs private

This project is summarized publicly only unless a dedicated public repo is added later.

## What this demonstrates

Data modeling, research tooling, AI-assisted discovery, and complex information architecture.
