# BurgReport

## Summary

BurgReport is a public build sample for fine-wine pricing intelligence and AI-assisted research workflows.

## Problem

Wine pricing and market research can require repeated manual lookup, normalization, and comparison across sources.

## What I built

A public build sample around pricing intelligence and AI-assisted research workflows.

## Stack

FastAPI, Supabase/PostgreSQL, Vercel/Next.js where applicable, and AI-assisted research workflows.

## What is public vs private

A public repo exists at https://github.com/Justlrnal4/burgreport.

BurgReport is one public build sample. It is not presented as my main project or the center of my work.

## What this demonstrates

Full-stack product build experience, data-backed workflow design, and applied AI research patterns.
