# Reztrix

## Summary

Reztrix is an AI hospitality operations platform focused on hotel operations workflows, structured knowledge, and practical AI support for operators.

## Problem

Hotel operations teams deal with fragmented knowledge, recurring guest and service workflows, and operational decisions spread across people, tools, and documents.

## What I built

Product direction, workflow design, and technical build work around structured hospitality operations knowledge and AI-assisted operator support.

## Stack

Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL/Supabase, pgvector, React/TypeScript, and AI workflow design.

## What is public vs private

This project is summarized publicly only. Private code, internal files, and detailed implementation materials are not exposed in this repo.

## What this demonstrates

Hospitality domain depth, product thinking, AI workflow design, and operator-first software judgment.
